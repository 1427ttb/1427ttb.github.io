ANGELICA ROSS
“I’ve always had big dreams and very little means.”

Angelica Ross was a businesswoman, actress, and transgender rights, advocate. She became a self-taught coder
early on and became the founder of TransTech Social Enterprises, a company that employs transgender people in the
tech industry. The “Foot Soldier” movie by Melissa Harris-Perry brought attention to the firm. Her achievements led
her to be featured at the 2015 White House LGBTQ Tech and Innovation Summit.
