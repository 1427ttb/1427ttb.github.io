INDRA NOOYI
“Leadership is hard to define and good leadership even harder. But if you can get people to follow you to the ends of the earth, you are a great leader.”

Indra Nooyi was born in 1955 in India. She attended school in India and started her career in India prior to attending
Yale School of Management. From there, she joined PepsiCo in 1994 and was named CEO in 2004; in later years, she appeared
on Wall Street Journal's top 50 women to watch in 2007 and 2008. She was ranked #2 in the Fortune 500 in 2015.
