GINNI ROMETTY
"You never let someone define who you are. Only you define who you are.”

Ginni Rometty, born in Illinois, is the current chairman, president, and CEO of IBM, and is currently ranked #34 in the Fortune
500. She joined IBM in 1981 as a series engineer, then led a series of leadership positions in the company prior to becoming CEO,
rising through the ranks. Hard work and diligence, along with her bravery to take risks allowed for her to take IBM and rise up
to new heights. Under her leadership, IBM received the Catalyst Award for advancing diversity and women’s initiatives. IBM was
the only tech company to receive this award in the past 20 years, and the only company to be recognized 4 times for it.
